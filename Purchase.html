Donations


The majority of hatcheries and conservation centers in Sri Lanka are operated by non-profit organizations in the private sector. These organizations heavily depend on donations to effectively protect and preserve turtles. Their initiatives play a crucial role in safeguarding nesting sites, mitigating risks such as bycatch and pollution, and spreading awareness about the significance of turtle conservation. Even a modest contribution can make a significant impact in safeguarding these magnificent sea creatures. Make a donation today and contribute towards their protection.
